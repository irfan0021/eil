FACILITY

The plant has an installed capacity to manufacture 80 Million rounds per annum, manufactured in NATO military standards.

EIL Arms And Ammunition Ltd has setup a highly advanced facility for Arms assembly for Assault Rifles, Hand Guns, Sniper and Precision Rifles as well as manufacturing small Caliber Ammunition, in Telangana Industry Area , Telangana. The facility is spread over 10 Acres and has an In-house test facility and range.
